Adding Crypto (Connecting Exchange/Custodial Wallets)

You can connect crypto exchanges and wallets to Exirio by selecting the '+' button and then "Connect Crypto Exchange". 

Following the on-screen instructions, you will be prompted to provide your credentials which are used - through a 3rd party provider (Zabo) - to access, obtain and provide your crypto data to Exirio. 

Zabo has no access to private keys, collets zero personal information on users who connect accounts, guarantees end-to-end encryptions on any credentials entering the system and military-grade security around backend infrastructure. 

Exirio never has access to your login credentials. 


Please note that the first time the connection may require a long time.
