For Exchanges and Wallets, Exirio will source and update daily their valuations. 

First, you need to give a name to your Exchange/Wallet, or select any of your existing ones. 

In the field marked Cryptocurrency, you can search for the specific product you want to add, and if if there is a public price source for it you will never have to update its valuation again.  

When you add an Investment with available public prices, Exirio will automatically suggest a transaction price (the closing price of the day the transaction was executed, or yesterday’s closing price if you are adding a transaction today), which of course you can edit and change into the actual price at which you have traded.
