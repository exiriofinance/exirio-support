Alternative ways to add Crypto

On Exirio, you can choose to add your cryptocurrencies in 3 ways:

  1. Connecting your Exchange/Wallet to your Exirio account, if the connection is supported by Exirio;
  2. As an Exchange or Wallet, replicating your individual Crypto positions in Exirio;
  3. Classifying your wallet of cryptocurrencies as a single Investment (with a cash and a crypto component).
