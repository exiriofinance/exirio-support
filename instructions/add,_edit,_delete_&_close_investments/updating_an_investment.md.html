Updating an Investment

You can update your Investments by adding Transactions and Valuations (if they are not automatically updated).

From your Home page, if you select Holdings you will find the full list of your holdings, and you can quickly edit some of their details. 

Or if you select the specific Investment on the menu on the left and access its details, you can add or view Transactions and Valuations (if they are not automatically updated) by selecting the corresponding tabs.
