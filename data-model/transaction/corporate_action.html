Corporate Action

Overview

A corporate action is an event initiated by a public company that affects the securities issued by the company. This can include actions such as dividends, stock splits, mergers, and acquisitions.

Types of Corporate Actions

  • Dividends: Payments made to shareholders from a company's earnings.
  • Stock Splits: An increase in the number of shares outstanding while reducing the par value of each share.
  • Mergers and Acquisitions: The consolidation of companies or assets.
  • Rights Issues: An offer to existing shareholders to purchase additional shares at a discount.

Impact on Shareholders

Corporate actions can significantly impact the value of shares and the overall investment strategy of shareholders. It is essential for investors to stay informed about these actions.
