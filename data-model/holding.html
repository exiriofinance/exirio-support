Holdings

In Exirio's data model, "Holdings" represent the fundamental structure for organizing and categorizing all your financial assets and liabilities. Holdings follow a hierarchical classification system that allows for precise tracking and analysis of your complete financial portfolio.

Holdings Structure

The Holdings structure in Exirio follows a three-level hierarchy:

  • Level 1: Holding Class - The broadest category (Investment, Cash, or Debt)
  • Level 2: Holding Type - Subcategories within each class
  • Level 3: Holding Subtype - Specific types of assets or liabilities

Holding Classes

Exirio organizes all holdings into three primary classes:

Investment

The "investment" class encompasses assets that have the potential to generate returns or appreciate in value. This includes:

  • Crypto: Digital currencies and tokens held in exchanges or wallets
  • Luxury Assets: High-value personal possessions like jewelry, art, and collectibles
  • Commodities: Physical goods like precious metals
  • Funds, Brokers & Banks: Traditional financial investments including securities portfolios and fund investments
  • Private Companies: Ownership stakes in non-public businesses
  • Real Estate: Property investments across residential, commercial, and land
  • Retirement Plans: Pension funds and retirement savings vehicles
  • Other: Miscellaneous investment types not covered by other categories

Cash

The "cash" class represents liquid assets that are readily available for spending or investment:

  • Current Accounts: Everyday banking accounts
  • Notes & Coins: Physical currency
  • Bank Accounts: Savings and deposit accounts
  • Other: Additional cash-equivalent assets

Debt

The "debt" class covers all financial obligations and liabilities:

  • Mortgages: Home and property loans
  • Credit Cards: Revolving credit balances
  • Personal Loans: Including student loans, car loans, and consumer loans
  • Other: Miscellaneous debt obligations

Using the Holdings Structure

When adding assets or liabilities to Exirio, you'll navigate through this hierarchical structure to properly categorize each Holding. This classification system enables:

  • Accurate Asset Allocation: Visualize how your wealth is distributed across different asset classes
  • Targeted Performance Analysis: Compare returns within specific categories
  • Comprehensive Net Worth Calculation: Account for all assets and liabilities in your financial picture
  • Customized Reporting: Generate insights based on specific holding types

For more detailed information on specific Holding types, please refer to the related articles in the Investments, Other Assets, and Debt sections.
