Investments

In Exirio's data model, Investments represent financial assets that have the potential to generate returns. Investments are a core component of the platform and include various asset classes such as:

  • Stocks and Equities
  • Bonds and Fixed Income
  • Real Estate
  • Cryptocurrencies
  • Precious Metals
  • Alternative Investments
  • Retirement and Pension Plans

Investment Structure

Each investment in Exirio is structured with the following key attributes:

  • Investment Type: Categorizes the investment (e.g., Stock, Bond, Real Estate)
  • Holding Class: Broader classification of the investment
  • Holding Type: Specific type within the class
  • Current Value: Present market value of the investment
  • Purchase Value: Original cost basis
  • Return Metrics: Performance indicators like MWR, TWR, and total gain
  • Transactions: Record of all financial activities related to the investment

Investment Tracking

Exirio provides comprehensive tracking for investments through:

  • Automatic Valuation: For publicly traded assets with available price sources
  • Manual Updates: For private or illiquid investments
  • Transaction Recording: Deposits, withdrawals, income, and expenses
  • Performance Analysis: Historical and comparative performance metrics

For more detailed information on specific investment types and how to manage them, please refer to the relevant articles in the Instructions section.
