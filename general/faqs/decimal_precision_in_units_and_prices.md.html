Decimal Precision in Units and Prices

Understanding how Exirio handles decimal precision is crucial for accurate financial tracking. We maintain high precision standards to ensure your financial data is represented accurately across different asset types and currencies.

Asset Units Precision

  • Cryptocurrencies: Up to 8 decimal places (e.g., 0.00000001 BTC)
  • Stocks and ETFs: Up to 6 decimal places for fractional shares
  • Commodities: Up to 3 decimal places (e.g., 0.001 oz of gold)
  • Real Estate: Up to 2 decimal places for partial ownership
  • Cash and Fiat: 2 decimal places, following standard currency format

Price Precision

  • Cryptocurrency prices: Up to 8 decimal places for small-value tokens
  • Stock prices: Up to 4 decimal places
  • Exchange rates: Up to 6 decimal places
  • Commodity prices: Up to 2 decimal places for standard units

Calculations and Rounding

  • All internal calculations maintain maximum precision to avoid rounding errors
  • Display values are rounded to appropriate decimals based on context
  • Totals and summaries use 2 decimal places for final display
  • Percentage calculations maintain 2 decimal places

For specific asset types or use cases requiring different precision levels, you can adjust the display settings in your account preferences. If you need more information about how we handle specific numerical cases, please contact our support team.
