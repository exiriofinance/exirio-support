Is my data secure?

At Exirio, we take data security very seriously. Here's how we protect your information:

Security Measures

  • Bank-level encryption for all data transmission and storage
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Multi-factor authentication (2FA) for account access
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Compliance with international data protection regulations

Data Privacy

  • Your data is never sold to third parties
  • Strict access controls and user permissions
  • Regular data backups with encrypted storage
  • Option to delete your account and data at any time

For more detailed information about our security practices and data protection policies, please visit our security page or review our privacy policy.
