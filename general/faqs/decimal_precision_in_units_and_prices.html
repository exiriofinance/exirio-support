Decimal precision in units and prices

Understanding how Exirio handles decimal precision is crucial for accurate financial tracking. We maintain high precision standards to ensure your financial data is represented accurately across different asset types and currencies.

There are some market standards that vary by instrument type, market, etc. and we default to those configurations. However, we also find that different Institutions might apply different unit and price precision for the exact same instrument. We can set up decimal precision for units and prices are for each individual public instrument so if you are not receiving the precision you need, let us know which instruments you need greater precision for (and for which variables) and we'll amend them.

Calculations and presentation rounding

  • All internal calculations maintain maximum configured precision to avoid rounding errors
  • Display values are rounded to appropriate decimals based on context
